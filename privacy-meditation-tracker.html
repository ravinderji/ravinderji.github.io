Privacy Policy

Meditation Tracker

1. Microphone Access

Our app requests the microphone permission (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO) only to detect and count mantras that you chant. All speech recognition is processed locally on your device using offline recognition models. We do not record, store, or transmit audio data.

2. Internet Access

Internet permission is used only for Google Transliteration API when you choose to transliterate text into Hindi. This is optional and only processes the text you provide. No audio or personal data is uploaded.

3. Data Collection & Sharing

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No data is stored on servers.
  • No information is shared with third parties.

4. Security

Since data is processed locally on your device, your information remains private and secure.
