About Meditation Tracker

Meditation Tracker is a simple yet powerful tool designed to enhance your meditation and chanting practice. Whether you are repeating a mantra, affirmation, or sacred phrase, this app helps you stay focused by counting your mantras using voice recognition — so you can immerse yourself in the experience instead of worrying about keeping track.

✨ Key Features

  • 🎤 Offline Voice Recognition – Counts mantras in real time without internet.
  • 📖 Google Transliteration – Easily add mantras in Hindi or English.
  • 🧘 Target Counts – Set goals like 27, 54, 108 or custom counts.
  • 🔔 Completion Alerts – Sound & vibration when your goal is reached.
  • 📊 Progress Tracking – See your chant counts and last used mantra.
  • 🌙 Modern UI – Material 3 design with light/dark mode support.

⚙️ How It Works

  1. Open the app and select or add a mantra.
  2. Set your target count (default: 108).
  3. Tap Start Chanting – the app listens and counts automatically.
  4. When you reach the target, you’ll get a completion alert.

🔒 Permissions Explained

  • 🎤 Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) – Only used to detect and count mantras locally. Audio is processed entirely on your device. No recording, saving, or sharing.
  • 🌐 Internet – Used only if you choose Google Transliteration (for typing mantras in Hindi). No personal data is transmitted.

✅ We do not collect, store, or share your data. See our Privacy Policy.
